PClassic 2014 Spring Summary

PClassic 2014s happened on April 26th, 2014. It saw 150 participants on over 40 teams from 20 schools participating, coming from Pennsylvania, Maryland, New Jersey, and New York.

Our runners-up from last semester returned to take gold in a photo-finish, submitting their final solution with just 10 seconds to spare!

The top 3 teams this year were:
1) Unaffiliated from Virginia - Daniel Hussain, Hyo Won Kim, Mathew Savage, Corwin De Boor
2) West Windsor Plainsboro HS North - Tanvi Bajpai, Manyu Sharma, Aaron Berger, Alok Tripathy
3) West Windsor Plainsboro HS North - Aaron Weiss, Benjamin Edelman, Jacob Edelman, Ernest Chiu

Congratulations to the winning teams! Prizes included trophies and free space on Dropbox!

Congratulations also to West Windsor Plainsboro HS North Team 0xFC0 (Mahesh Khanwalkar) for winning the novice division!
